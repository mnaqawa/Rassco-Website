Who We Are

RASSCO Group operates as a dynamic platform uniting diverse capabilities across vital sectors, guided by one vision: Sustainable Growth & Creating the Future.

Established in Riyadh, the group is structured into four specialized divisions, Logistics, Healthcare, Technology & Cybersecurity and Investment.

Today, the organization serves 80+ prestigious clients across the Kingdom, supported by 10+ subsidiaries, all driven by one purpose: empowering businesses to reach their full potential.

Ideas transform into leading companies, and investments turn into tangible achievements — shaping tomorrow across the Kingdom.
