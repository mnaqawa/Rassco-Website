RASSCO Group

RASSCO Group is a proud member of the RAS Capital ecosystem — a hub uniting diverse companies across vital sectors under one vision: Sustainable Growth & Creating the Future.

Established in Riyadh, RASSCO operates through four specialized subgroups — Workforce, Logistics, Healthcare, and Technology — each built to address the unique demands of its sector while delivering the same RASSCO standard of excellence.

Today, RASSCO serves 80+ prestigious clients across the Kingdom, with 10+ subsidiaries, driven by one purpose: empowering businesses to reach their full potential.
